Bio

I am a software engineer passionate about Human-Inspired artificial intelligence, specializing in natural language processing (NLP) and user interface (UI) design. My work centers around creating interactive experiences powered by intelligent systems.

Currently, I am a software engineer at Stellar Cyber where I work on AI-powered systems for threat hunting and autonomous security operations. Additionally, I'm developing GONEXT, a GenAI tool that delivers personalized, game-specific analytics for League of Legends.

This fall, I will begin my Master of Philosophy in Human-Inspired Artificial Intelligence at the University of Cambridge. I hold a bachelor's degree from Stony Brook University, where I double-majored in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics & Statistics.
