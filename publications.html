EMF in Ellipses    PDF

A numerical approach developed for accurately calculating the motional electromotive force (EMF) induced in elliptical loops as they move within a uniform magnetic field, featuring adjustable error tolerance for enhanced precision.

P-M Binder, Kostadin G Devedzhiev, Alexandra T Runyan. (2020). Motional emf generated by squeezing an elliptical conducting loop. European Journal of Physics, European Physical Society. https://dx.doi.org/10.1088/1361-6404/abb066
